I'm Nandan — most people online know me as the techie who "hacked" an airline website to get his bag back. Off Twitter, I'm a software engineer with 10+ years of experience, and I consult with early-stage startups on product and engineering.

I currently lead architecture and security at Baggage Media, and I'm CEO/Director at Pawrivarik Technologies, where we help startups cut hiring turnaround time. Before that, I spent five years at o9 Solutions building UI infrastructure, dark mode, and NLP tooling for their planning platform.

My claim to fame was a 2022 Twitter thread about a data privacy flaw on an airline's website that went viral for the right reasons — covered by BBC, Times of India, Boing Boing, Lallantop, and others, and picked up by radio and podcasts around the world.

Off-screen: photography, gardening, snooker, and boxing. I own a lot of plants and I talk to them (mostly pep talks).
